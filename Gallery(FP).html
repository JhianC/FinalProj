Poetic Justice

Seeing The World Through Words

Foreword

Indulge yourself with photography and poetry which range from english and tagalog verses that aims to connect you with your inner thoughts and spiritual being. Stoics and philosophers from the earlier era's of our world view poetry as a medium for their philosophical views in life whilst also teaching the readers about the facts of life and the somewhat harsh reality of the world we live in. What better way to enjoy poetry and photography than enabling your wanderlust side. So sit back, relax, and let your eyes and mind travel through your imagination.

Self Reflection

To The Clouds Above

Seeking up above for something that you cannot find on the ground before you as you gaze your eyes and ponder.

Do you ever wonder? Think about yourself and yourself alone as you let your mind wander?

How often do you let yourself dream about what's coming and where you're headed.

Often we resort to only what we know within our minds as it is what's already embedded.

Step out of what's keeping you inside. Only then you'll truly experience the unexpected.

Still

As still as the tree as you are today. As still as the tree on a clear and sunny day.

No matter how strong the breeze, you'll never break but only sway. No matter the breeze you won't break away.

Keep the branches close to you, but never be afraid to lose them. Like wounds, they heal but leave a scar as expected.

No matter how high you reach and grow, remain low and rooted. The moment you go high up, you'll become imputed.

As sturdy as the tree, no matter the obstacle you'll be okay. As sturdy as the tree, you'll always find a way.

Home is Where Your Heart Is

I want to grow but I never want to leave you behind. The cool breeze is what I'll miss the most.

In a circumstance where another path is what I'll find. I'll be glancing at your from another coast.

Memories were made in the city of pines. From the city lights at night and to all the streets I've roamed.

The day I leave will not be the final goodbye. There won't be "so long's" as this will not be the end of the road.

My home is where my heart lies, it is where I grow and where I thrive. My home is where I woke.

Epiphany

Do you believe in the law of attraction? Set your mind into something and it will eventually be yours.

The mere belief isn't enough as it is only a fraction. Shortcuts aren't valid, it's necessary to make detours.

In this world we live in, the mind is truly powerful. But without action, the results won't be as wonderful.

How can you achieve what you want if you aren't masterful? Delusions of grandeur will be your funeral.

The mind wants what the heart desires. It makes every person feel bliss.

But the desire is a thought alone. So it's necessary to take risks.

Self Discovery

Unearthed

Each step you take will take you to where you should be. The ground you walk upon decides the fate of an entire lifetime.

No matter the hurdles, believe on what you could be. As in this life you're not handed any lifelines.

Search from deep within your mind and soul. Unearth who you truly want to be.

Focus on the tasks and your personal goal. A complete life is what's waiting for you to see..

Knowledge is Power

Ignorance kills the mind's ability to think freely. It locks it and makes it feel weary.

Allow your mind to take in things completely. Fortify it with information routinely.

As our minds serve as our greatest weapon equipped in our daily lives. It's what helps us thrive.

Sharpen your mind, let your creativity flow. Never forget the grind, and surely the future will glow.

Serenity

The peace felt within the soul when you see where the land meets the sea. Hearing the waves crash stimulates the serotonin.

The white sand that covers my feet fills me with glee. The motion of the ocean plays with my emotion.

A state of zen is felt when you find time to appreciate the world. Each and everything the eyes see are purposeful.

Clear your mind and let yourself be undisturbed. As inner peace is also quite powerful.

Optimism & Resilience

Pag-Ugoy

Balik tanaw sa mga nakaraan upang makalayag papunta sa paroroonan. Mabagal man ang pag-usad, tiyak ang pinatutunguhan.

Kahit pa gaano na katagal ang nakaraang dumaan, lahat ito'y may aral at mahalagang leksyon na pinapasan.

Sabay lang sa pag ugoy ng buhay ng iyong malaman. Na ang basehan ng buhay ay hindi nadadaan sa pabilisan.

Makaluma

Lumang bahay na nakatayo sa isang kanto. Nagdaan na ang panahon ngunit ang paglumaan ito'y malabo.

Lumipas man ang dekada't mga taon ay hindi ito malilimutan. Bagkus kapag napangalagaa'y tiyak may karoroonan.

Maaring maihalintulad ito sa ating mga sarili. Kung ano man ang iyong pundasyon, yun ang tiyak na mananatili.

Pueblo

O' pueblong pinanggalingan nawa'y manatili lahat ng mga natutunang pangagaral sa buhay

O' pueblong pinanggalingan aking kinagisnang maranasang sa panahon ng kahirapan ikay' maging karamay.

O' pueblong babalikan, ika'y tanging sa damdamin ko'y bumubuhay. Mula sa puso't utak dala hanggang hukay.

Bahaghari

Maulang hapon nanaman muli, damdami'y hindi tiyak at padalos dalos ang nararamdaman.

Kasing dilim ng mga ulap ang mga pag-aatubili. Makulimlim at para bang walang sandalan.

Ngunit manatiling pinapalakas ang iyong sarili. Pagka't sa dulo ng ulan ay may bahaghari.

Patience is a Virtue

Baybay

Tulad ng mga bato na hinahampas ng alon sa dalampasigan, ikaw ba'y mananatili o sasabay na rin sa paglisan

Saan man mahatak ng tadhana'y sumunod lang sa iyong isipan. Ikaw at ikaw lamang ang bangkang magdadala sa'yong mga inaasam.

Luna

Takipsilim ang bumungad at nagpapakilala sa mga bituin at buwan. Dilim na sumusonod ay nagiging maliwanag dahil sa'yong ilaw.

Gaano man kadilim ang kalsadang dadaanan. Walang dilim na ang hindi malalampasan gaano man kalalim o kababaw.

Buhangin

Ang mga buhangin ang madalas gamitin sa orasa upang sukatin ang isang oras na dumaraan.

Bali-baliktarin lamang at ang oras na hindi mo nabibilang ay iyo nang mahahawakan.

Pasensya

Buhay ay sadyang mabagal at ang pag-usad nito'y nakakainip at nakakatuliro.

Manatiling tapat sa sariling mga panaginip at misyon. Huwag kang matakot maiwan at mabigo.
